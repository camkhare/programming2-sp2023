Instructions

  1. The objective of blackjack is to get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand value without going over 21.
  2. Players are dealt two cards, and the dealer is dealt one card face up and one card face down.
  3. Players can choose to "hit" (get another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand) until they are satisfied with their hand value or go over 21 (bust).
  4. The dealer must hit until their hand value is 17 or higher, and then must stand.
  5. If the player's hand value is higher than the dealer's and does not exceed 21, the player wins. If the dealer's hand value is higher than the player's, the player loses.
  6. Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points, and numbered cards are worth their face value.
  7. If the player's initial two cards are an ace and a 10-point card, they have a blackjack and automatically win 1.5 times their original bet.
